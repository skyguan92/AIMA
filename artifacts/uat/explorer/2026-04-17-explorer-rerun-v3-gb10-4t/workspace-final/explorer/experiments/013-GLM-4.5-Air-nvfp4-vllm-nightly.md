## Agent Notes

GLM-4.5-Air-nvfp4 reliably serves single-request inference across 128-, 512-, and 16384-token inputs with stable TPOT (~43–55 ms). Throughput halves at 16K context (11.7 TPS vs ~22.8 TPS at shorter lengths) due to prefill overhead, and TTFT jumps to ~7.7 s, which is expected for a 57.68 GiB model. This confirms the model can handle its rated 16K context window on vllm-nightly when constrained to concurrency=1.
