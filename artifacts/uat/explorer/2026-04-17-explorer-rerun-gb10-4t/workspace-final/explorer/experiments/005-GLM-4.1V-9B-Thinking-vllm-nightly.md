All 6/6 cells passed, confirming vllm-nightly scales successfully to larger 19.17 GiB GLM models. Peak throughput 10.2 TPS with very stable TPOT P95 (~98-99 ms) across all input lengths. TTFT scales linearly with prompt length, reaching ~996 ms at 4096 tokens. Compared to GLM-4.6V-Flash-FP4 (8.25 GiB), throughput is ~3.2× lower, which is reasonable given the 2.3× size increase. This validates that vllm-nightly is not limited to small models and handles both Qwen and GLM families correctly on GB10.
